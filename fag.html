Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions answered, right here!

What are your delivery times?
Our delivery times are between 3-5 working days for local orders and 7-10 working days for international orders.
Do you deliver on holidays?
No, we do not work during the holidays. Your item will be delivered to you after the holiday period
Can I cancel my order?
Yes, you can cancel your order within 24 hours of placement. Please contact our support team.
How do I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, we will provide you with a tracking number via email.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.
What is your return policy?
You can return items within 30 days of receipt, provided they are in their original condition.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we offer international shipping to select countries. Additional fees may apply.
