Our Policies

Return and Exchange Policy

We strive to ensure that our customers are satisfied with their purchases. However, if you are not satisfied with an item, you can return it within 14 days of receipt. Items must be in their original condition, unused, and accompanied by the receipt.

Exchanges are allowed only for defective or damaged items. Please contact our customer service at support@example.com to initiate a return or exchange process.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. We collect personal information only to process your orders and provide a better shopping experience. This includes your name, email address, phone number, and shipping address.

We do not share your information with third parties, except as necessary to fulfill your orders. Our website uses secure encryption to protect your data.

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at privacy@example.com.

Shipping Policy

We offer fast and reliable shipping services. Orders are processed within 1-2 business days and shipped via our trusted courier partners. Delivery times vary depending on your location.

Shipping fees are calculated based on the delivery address and order total. Orders over $50 qualify for free shipping.

For international shipping, additional customs fees or taxes may apply. These charges are the responsibility of the customer.
