Frequently Asked Questions

What is this website about?

▾

This website is a dedicated online shop for students of Osun State University, offering an easy and reliable way to purchase essential items at affordable prices.

How do I place an order?

▾

Browse the available products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout to complete your order. It's quick and simple!

Do you offer delivery services?

▾

Yes, we deliver items directly to your hostel or residence. We also offer free delivery for orders above a specific amount.

What payment methods are accepted?

▾

We accept bank transfers as our primary payment method. Details are provided during checkout.

Can I return or exchange an item?

▾

Yes, we allow returns or exchanges within 7 days of delivery. The item must be in its original condition. Please visit our Returns and Exchanges page for more details.
